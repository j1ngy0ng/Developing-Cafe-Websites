At I Like Tea Tea Coffee Shop, we believe that every cup of coffee tells a story. Our story began with a simple dream - to create a warm and cozy space where people could enjoy their coffee comfortably.

In 2023, our cozy cafe quickly became a beloved gathering spot in the heart of Antarctica. From the moment you step through our doors, you'll be enveloped by the aroma of freshly brewed coffee beans, sourced from the finest coffee regions around the world.

Our team of talented baristas is dedicated to the art of coffee-making, and their passion is evident in every latte art they create and every espresso shot they pull. Whether you prefer a classic cappuccino, a velvety smooth flat white, or a bold and robust Americano, we've got something to satisfy every coffee lover's palate.

Whether you're seeking a quiet spot to work or study, catching up with friends, or simply seeking a moment of respite in your day, I Like Tea Tea is the perfect destination. Our welcoming ambiance, friendly staff, and soul-nourishing offerings create an experience that goes beyond just coffee - it's a heartfelt journey.
